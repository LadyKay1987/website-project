Hello everyone

Walkies is partnered with local dog shelters throughout Scotland to help encourage people to get more active and also more social. For dog enthusiasts who don't own a dog, you won't be left out: our shelters have lots of dogs who would love to meet you and go for walkies with you!

By walking our shelter dogs, you're not just getting more active and social, but you're also helping boost the chances of our dogs finding their forever home. Meeting new people and being around other dogs greatly helps our shelter dogs become used to people again; please get in touch with us for more information!

 

Hope to hear from you all soon!

- Everyone at the Walkies team
